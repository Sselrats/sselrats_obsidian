# 1. Neural Networks

  

## 1.1 Single Neuron

  

입력 $u \in \mathbb{R}^m$

  

$$

\phi(u) = \sigma(w^\top u + b)

$$

  

- $w \in \mathbb{R}^m$ : weight vector\

- $b \in \mathbb{R}$ : bias\

- $\sigma : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ : nonlinear activation function

  

학습 목표: $\theta = (w, b)$ 추정

  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

  

## 1.2 Layer

  

입력 $u \in \mathbb{R}^n$

  

$$

h = \sigma(Wu + b)

$$

  

- $W \in \mathbb{R}^{k \times n}$

- $b \in \mathbb{R}^k$

- $\sigma$ element-wise 적용

  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

  

## 1.3 Feedforward Neural Network

  

입력 $x \in \mathbb{R}^d$

  

$$

h^{(0)} = x

$$

  

$$

h^{(\ell)} = \sigma\!\left(W^{(\ell)} h^{(\ell-1)} + b^{(\ell)}\right),

\quad \ell = 1, \dots, L-1

$$

  

최종 출력:

  

$$

f_\Theta(x)

=

W^{(L)} h^{(L-1)} + b^{(L)}

$$

  

전체 파라미터:

  

$$

\Theta

=

\left\{

W^{(\ell)}, b^{(\ell)}

\right\}_{\ell=1}^L

$$

  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

  

# 2. Learning Setup

  

## Data

  

$$

S = \{(x_i, y_i)\}_{i=1}^n \sim \mathcal{D}

$$

  

IID 가정.

  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

  

## Risk

  

### Population Risk

  

$$

R(f)

=

\mathbb{E}_{(X,Y)\sim \mathcal{D}}

\big[

\ell(f(X), Y)

\big]

$$

  

### Empirical Risk

  

$$

\widehat{R}(f)

=

\frac{1}{n}

\sum_{i=1}^n

\ell(f(x_i), y_i)

$$

  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

  

# 3. Error Decomposition

  

$$

\text{Total Error}

=

\varepsilon_{\text{gen}}

+

\varepsilon_{\text{opt}}

+

\varepsilon_{\text{app}}

$$

  

- Approximation error

- Optimization error

- Generalization error

  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

  

# 4. Curse of Dimensionality

  

선형:

  

$$

f^*(x) = \langle x, \theta^* \rangle

$$

  

$n \sim d$

  

Lipschitz:

  

$$

|f(a) - f(b)| \le \beta \|a-b\|

$$

  

$$

\|x - x_{i}^*\| \sim n^{-1/d}

$$

  

$$

\text{MSE} \sim n^{-2/d}

$$
